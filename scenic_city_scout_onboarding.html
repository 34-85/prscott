3 · The contest mechanic & build notes
  • Prize: each month, every completed entry is in a draw for a $50 gift card to a business we've featured — the prize itself promotes a local maker. One winner, announced in-issue (good content + goodwill).
  • Bonus entries: refer a friend (beehiiv referral link) = +1 entry. Stacks growth onto the data capture.
  • beehiiv setup: add Q1–Q5 as custom signup fields (single/multi-select). Keep it to 5 to protect completion. If a field type is limited, ask one question at a time via the welcome automation (progressive profiling).
  • Automation: trigger the welcome email on signup; tag completers as scout-insider for the monthly draw and for segmenting sends.
  • Don't re-ask. Later, add one new profiling question per issue (progressive profiling) — never a wall of forms. Frequency cap protects the list.
  • Privacy: link a real privacy policy; honor “data” / unsubscribe immediately; first-party only — do not buy third-party appends. Aggregate-only, no PII to anyone.
