# Part 1 — One-Page Pilot Brief

**Why — and how it fits APPA's mission (the Rosetta Stone):** APPA already spends **~$2.5M/year** on mission work that depends on local community/connectivity but has **no local delivery channel.** This pilot proves PoC can be that channel — turning **advocacy from an expense into a profit center** by solving pet-care's biggest problems (declining vet visits, pet-inclusive housing, independent-retail/live-sales threats, stalled ownership, member-brand local connection) *and making money doing it.* Mission and margin pull the same direction.

**What:** A lean, ~5-month pilot of **Pets of [City]** — a hyper-local pet **utility + community + data** platform (weekly newsletter as the funnel; directory, adoption, housing, events, discount card, data panel) — in **4 Southeast markets: Chattanooga (HQ/lead), Brentwood/Franklin (member-brand HQ hub), Columbia, Raleigh.**

**Why now:** Validate the riskiest assumptions cheaply **before** incorporating APPA Ventures or committing the staged ~$3M. A weak pilot saves ~$3M and a year.

**Scope & structure:** Internal APPA program (no entity); AI-assisted, human-edited, disclosed content; high-margin-line + platform-seed tests concentrated in **Chattanooga (HQ)**; consent/panel opt-in from day one.

**Budget:** ~$200K (operating-budget line; under any entity-formation threshold).

**What we're testing:**
- **H1 Demand** (cheap, engaged subscribers) · **H2 Engagement** (opens/reads) · **H3 Monetization — high-margin lines** (insurance lead-gen, operator-side housing, paid lead-gen) · **H4 Ops & quality** (AI+human, one editor) · **H5 Platform-seed** (discount card, event, product-JV, panel opt-in)
- **Mission-amplification** (the "why"): vet-wellness-visit promotion, vet/shelter/independent-retailer/housing participation, advocacy reach.

**Go decision:** GO to incorporate + fund if **≥3 of 4 markets** clear subscriber + engagement thresholds **and** monetization is validated in **≥1 market** including **≥1 high-margin line** (not ads alone). H5 + mission signals strengthen the case but don't gate. (Full thresholds: Part 3.)

**The ask:** Approve the **~$200K pilot budget** and a **fractional project lead + Chattanooga (HQ) editor**, with a board decision gate at ~Month 5.

# Part 3 — KPI Dashboard (business + mission)

Track per market and overall; review weekly (leading) and at the Month-5 gate (decision). Targets per market by end of pilot unless noted.

### A. Business — audience & engagement
| KPI | Target | How measured |
|---|---|---|
| Subscribers | 3,000–5,000 | Platform |
| Subscriber CAC | ≤ $1.50 | Spend ÷ net adds |
| Open rate | ≥ 40% | Platform |
| Click rate | ≥ 3% | Platform |
| Unsubscribe / send | < 0.5% | Platform |
| **Engagement durability** | Open rate holds (no steep decay) | Cohort trend (fatigue read) |
| **Panel opt-in** | ≥ 25–40% of subs | Consent flow |

### B. Business — monetization (high-margin lines first)
| KPI | Target | How measured |
|---|---|---|
| **Insurance lead-gen** | Measurable leads at viable effective payout ($25–152/policy benchmark) | Affiliate dashboard |
| **Operator-side housing** | ≥ 2–3 property managers express paid interest | Sales pipeline |
| **Paid directory / pay-per-lead** | Paying vet/groomer advertisers; ~$40/lead benchmark | CRM |
| Member-brand sponsor | ≥ 1 national sponsor signed/committed | Sales |
| Discount card | ≥ 10–20 businesses + consumer sign-ups + first redemptions | Card platform |
| Event | ≥ breakeven with ≥ 1 paying sponsor | Event P&L |
| Product-JV starter kit | Sells with ≥ 1 member-brand partner | Pilot sales |

### C. Mission-amplification (the "why")
| KPI | Target | How measured |
|---|---|---|
| **Vet wellness visits driven** *(headline)* | Track # wellness-visit **redemptions/bookings** via card + trackable offers; + self-reported via panel | Card redemptions + offer links + panel survey |
| **Vet participation** | ≥ 5–10 local vets in directory/card/lead-gen | Partner roster |
| **Shelter/adoption engagement** | Weekly adoptable features; adoptions/clicks driven; ≥ 2–3 shelter partners | Shelter partners + click tracking |
| **Independent-retailer participation** | ≥ 5–10 independents featured/in card | Partner roster |
| **Housing-operator interest** | ≥ 2–3 operators engaged (ties to §B housing line) | Pipeline |
| **Advocacy reach / mobilization** | Local pet-owner audience reachable for advocacy (size + engagement) | List + engagement |
| Member-brand local connection | ≥ 1 member brand activated locally | Sponsor/JV |

### D. Go/No-Go scorecard (decision rule)
- **GO** if: **≥ 3 of 4 markets** clear **A** (subscribers + open/click) **AND** **≥ 1 market** validates **B** including **≥ 1 high-margin line** (insurance / housing / lead-gen) **AND** zero factual/YMYL incidents.
- **Strengtheners (non-gating):** strong **C** mission signals (esp. vet wellness visits), panel opt-in ≥ target, card/event/JV traction.
- **Otherwise:** iterate (pricing/markets/lines) or stop — cheaply.
