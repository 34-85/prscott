## What "no restrictions" really means (and why it's rare)

True "no restrictions" = **no breed list + no weight/size cap + no aggressive number cap.** Almost nothing is fully unrestricted — most "pet-friendly" or even "no breed restriction" communities still: cap at **2 pets**, charge **pet fees/deposit + monthly pet rent**, and may keep an **insurance/"aggressive animal" clause**. The closest thing to "no restrictions" is a **management-company policy** that drops breed bans across a whole portfolio and substitutes a **pet interview**.

## Important caveats (read before using any of this)

1. **Sources are commercial and change fast.** Apartment-locator blogs earn referral fees; property marketing is sales copy. A "no breed restriction" claim from 2025–26 may be outdated by the time of publication.
2. **"No breed restriction" ≠ "no weight limit" ≠ "no pet cap."** These are three separate policies; confirm all three.
3. **Fees almost always still apply** — typical Austin ranges seen in sources: one-time pet fee ~$250–$400, pet rent ~$15–$20/mo/pet; deposits vary.
4. **Insurance/"aggressive animal" clauses** can override a "no breed restriction" headline.
5. **Within a management company, individual properties can vary** despite a stated portfolio policy.

---

## Verification protocol (before anything is published or listed)

For each property, confirm **in writing** (email/leasing portal) and log:
1. Breed restrictions — none, or specific list?
2. Weight/size cap — none, or limit (lbs)?
3. Pet-count cap (e.g., 2)?
4. Fees: one-time pet fee, deposit (refundable?), monthly pet rent per pet.
5. Any insurance / "aggressive breed" / individual-approval clause?
6. **Date verified** + staff name. **Re-verify quarterly** (policies drift).

Only properties that pass verification — and ideally that are paying directory partners — should appear in a published Pets of Austin housing listing. For editorial, cite only the **aggregate, sourced** stat (e.g., ~91% of TX rentals accept pets — Michelson Found Animals), never an unverified per-property claim.
